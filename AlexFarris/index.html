About ALEX

Alex Farris has been playing guitar since he was 7 years old. Since then, he has found his passion for drums, bass, piano, singing, and anything else he can get his hands on. Over the years, Alex has played in many bands from originals to covers of 80’s and 90’s rock n’ roll. He has composed music for independent rap artists around the Sacramento area and for independent filmmakers.

Along the way, he has found his biggest passion to be in acting. Performing in pieces ranging from commercials to feature films.

Grateful for the ability to follow his dreams and pursue his passions, Alex works hard every day and strives to be the best he can be, hoping his art will bring as much emotion to others as it has to him.
